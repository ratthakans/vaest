The short version

If VÆST is not what you needed, write to rakan@orions.agency within 7 days of a charge and we refund it in full. You do not have to explain yourself, and having already made documents does not forfeit it — we would rather you tried the thing properly than held back to protect a refund.

How to ask

  • Email rakan@orions.agency from the address on the account.
  • We reply within one business day and process the refund through Stripe.
  • Stripe returns it to the original card. Banks usually post it within 5–10 business days — that part is out of our hands.

Cancelling instead

Cancelling and refunding are different things. You can cancel any time from Settings ▸ Usage ▸ Change plan, which opens the billing portal. Cancelling stops the next charge and keeps your access until the end of the period you have already paid for — nothing is cut off the moment you click.

Your documents stay in your account after cancelling. If you want them gone as well, Settings ▸ Privacy exports everything and deletes the account outright.

After 7 days

Past the window we do not refund a month already under way, and we do not pro-rate a partial month on cancellation — cancel before the renewal date and you are not charged again.

That said, if something on our side went wrong — an engine failed, a document you paid for never arrived, a charge you did not expect — write to us regardless of the date. A refund window is for changing your mind, not for absorbing our mistakes.

Usage packs

Usage packs (฿490) are consumable and non-refundable once any part has been spent. Unspent packs fall under the same 7-day window as a subscription charge.

VAT and tax invoices

Prices include VAT. ORIONS Creative Co., Ltd. is VAT-registered (0105568063442), and the 7% is already inside the figure you see — ฿390 is ฿364.49 plus ฿25.51 of VAT, and nothing is added at checkout. A tax invoice (ใบกำกับภาษี) showing that split is available on request: write to us with the billing name and address you need on it. A refund returns the whole charge, VAT included.

Your statutory rights

Nothing here reduces the rights you have under Thai consumer law. Where this policy and the law disagree, the law wins.
