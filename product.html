Phase 1 · Idea

Idea

The sparring partner.

Gemini FlashSandboxFallback: Haiku 4.5

What it is · how it works

  • A fast, generous sandbox on the home screen — throw sparks, options, and raw pastes from any other model.
  • It riffs long when the idea deserves it, keeps the good part, kills the weak part and says why.
  • Any reply can be promoted “→ brief” as a tile; the whole thread feeds Summing as curated raw material.
  • Runs on Gemini Flash for speed and low cost; if it ever hiccups, it falls back to Haiku automatically — it never breaks.
Phase 2 · Ø Think

Ø Think

The senior creative director.

Claude Opus 4.8Provocation pass

What it is · how it works

  • The signature move. Opus reads the whole canvas like a CD reviewing a junior's deck — not proofreading, pushing.
  • Finds the braver version: missing cultural hooks, safe choices to bend (minimalist → a brutalist clash), angles that stop one step early.
  • Each push is anchored to an exact quote in the document and highlighted in place — click a card to jump to the spot.
  • Approve the pushes you like; VÆST writes them in and remembers your taste from every decision.
Phase 3 · Refine

Refine

The final taste check.

Claude Fable 5Holistic audit

What it is · how it works

  • A bird's-eye read of the entire document, on the model most sensitive to tone and coherence.
  • Hunts three things: inconsistency (claims that contradict across sections), redundancy (ideas that circle), logic (does the flow hold up).
  • Every finding quotes the exact line and highlights it — a track-changes style card you approve one at a time or all at once.
  • The last read before the work leaves the studio.
Hand off · Present

Present

The deck builder.

Claude Sonnet 5Presentation

What it is · how it works

  • Reshapes the finished document into a presentation — cover, content, quote and close slides.
  • Sonnet plans the slides; VÆST renders them in its own night-prismatic look (never model-authored HTML).
  • Choose landscape 16:9 or portrait A4, then save as PDF from the print dialog. Thai and English supported.
