GALDR

The sung spell that sets the spark — and sharpens it.
Engine GALDR v3.0Idea & ThinkPropose-only
Galdr was the old north's sung magic — words chanted until they took shape in the world. VÆST's GALDR does the same for your ideas, at both ends of the work. At the start it is a living sandbox: it recites options back, argues art theory with you, catches half-thoughts and raw fragments pasted from anywhere, and flows — throwing sparks freely until the first true idea lands. Then, once ODIN has written the draft, the same mind returns as Think — reading a section like a CD reviewing a junior's deck, not proofreading but provoking: the missing cultural hook, the safe choice worth bending, the idea that stops one step short. It only ever proposes. Because it is a different engine from the one that wrote the page, the counsel is honest — and ODIN, not GALDR, writes any change.

What it does · how it works

  • Idea sandbox — a fast, generous brainstorm on the home screen: riff, debate, paste raw source from outside.
  • Keeps the good part, kills the weak part, and says why — then curates the thread into Crystallize. Promote any line with “→ brief.”
  • Think, per section — hit Think on any section and pushes appear right inside it; nothing gets lost between sections.
  • Approve / Skip per push — approve and ODIN rewrites just that section; every call feeds taste memory.
  • Speaks your chosen persona and language throughout. If it ever stumbles, a backup voice carries on — GALDR never falls silent.
Crystallize · Synthesis

ODIN

The all-seeing mind that brings order.
Engine ODIN v2.0Crystallize · Multi-canvasThe voice
Odin traded an eye for wisdom and hung nine nights to grasp the runes. VÆST's ODIN is the all-seeing mind that turns a mountain of input into order. When ideas settle, ODIN takes over — reading every chat, file and note, crystallizing chaos into one clean document, splitting distinct deliverables into their own canvases. Every word that lands on the canvas is his: one document, one voice — even when the counsel comes from elsewhere.

What it does · how it works

  • Crystallize — chats, brief, files and sparks become one sharp, sectioned document, streamed live.
  • Multi-canvas — identity vs copy vs event split into tabs, each standing on its own.
  • The writing hand — every edit, every approved push, every applied fix passes through ODIN, so the voice never fractures.
Refine · Present · The apex

NORRSKEN

The apex light — nothing ships until it has passed.
Engine NORRSKEN v3.2Apex · flagship-classFinal authority
Norrsken is the northern lights — the highest light in the sky. VÆST's NORRSKEN stands at the top of the chain: the apex judgment nothing escapes, the last word before the work leaves the studio. Built on our most powerful engine, it is the most sensitive read of all: NORRSKEN scans the entire document from above, hunting inconsistency, redundancy and broken logic. It doesn't rewrite blindly — it shows each flaw exactly where it lives and offers a change to approve, polishing the work to an editorial, pixel-clean finish before it reaches the client.

What it does · how it works

  • A bird's-eye audit for inconsistency (tone that contradicts), redundancy (ideas that circle), and logic (does the flow hold).
  • Every finding quotes the exact line and highlights it in the document — nothing hidden.
  • Track-changes style: approve one at a time or all at once; view exactly what moved.
  • When the document becomes a deck, NORRSKEN lands that too — the same apex judgment shapes the final presentation.
  • The last light before the work leaves the studio.
