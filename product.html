Phase 1 · Idea · Sandbox

SKALD

The poet who sets the spark.
Engine SKALD v2.5Sparking IdeaPersona-aware
In the old courts, the skald was the poet who turned history, culture and raw feeling into verse. VÆST's SKALD does the same for your ideas. This is where the work begins — a living sandbox that recites options back, argues art theory with you, catches half-thoughts and raw fragments pasted from anywhere. It doesn't judge yet; it flows — throwing sparks freely until the first true idea lands.

What it does · how it works

  • A fast, generous brainstorm mode on the home screen — riff, debate, paste raw source from outside.
  • Keeps the good part, kills the weak part, and says why — then curates the thread into Summing.
  • Promote any line with “→ brief.” Speaks your chosen persona and language on every reply.
  • Tuned for speed and flow; if it ever stumbles, a backup voice carries on — SKALD never falls silent.
Phase 2 · Think · Synthesis

ODIN

The all-seeing mind that brings order.
Engine ODIN v1.8Summing · Multi-canvasØ Think
Odin traded an eye for wisdom and hung nine nights to grasp the runes. VÆST's ODIN is the all-seeing mind that turns a mountain of input into order. When ideas settle, ODIN takes over — reading every file, crystallizing chaos into one clean document, splitting distinct deliverables into their own canvases. And when you press Ø Think, ODIN wakes as a Senior Creative and rips the work open, injecting master-level provocations to make it braver.

What it does · how it works

  • Summing — brief, files and ideas become one sharp, sectioned document, streamed live.
  • Multi-canvas — identity vs copy vs event split into tabs, each standing on its own.
  • Ø Think — a CD-grade pass: missing cultural hooks, safe choices to bend, angles that stop too early — each anchored to the exact line.
  • Learns your studio's taste from every push you approve or skip.
Phase 3 · Refine · Quality gate

NORRSKEN

The light that sweeps the work clean.
Engine NORRSKEN v3Editorial criticSuggest & approve
Norrsken is the northern lights — the sky sweeping the dark clean. VÆST's NORRSKEN is the final light that passes over the whole canvas before it ships. The most sensitive read of all: NORRSKEN scans the entire document from above, hunting inconsistency, redundancy and broken logic. It doesn't rewrite blindly — it shows each flaw exactly where it lives and offers a change to approve, polishing the work to an editorial, pixel-clean finish before it reaches the client.

What it does · how it works

  • A bird's-eye audit for inconsistency (tone that contradicts), redundancy (ideas that circle), and logic (does the flow hold).
  • Every finding quotes the exact line and highlights it in the document — nothing hidden.
  • Track-changes style: approve one at a time or all at once; view exactly what moved.
  • The last light before the work leaves the studio.
