VÆST 1.3
July 2026
Current

The Creative Engines

  • New
    Mimir · the counsel — Ø Think becomes its own mind, deliberately separate from the one that writes. It reads each section and pushes back; you approve, Odin writes. And the Idea room now holds several topic chats — each one a source for Crystallize, each saveable as .md.
  • New
    Galdr · Odin · Mimir · Norrsken · Skadi — five engines, each named for the old north, each with its own character and version. One flow of taste.
  • New
    Skadi · the huntress — the Present engine becomes its own named mind: it reads the finished document from the summit and lands it as an on-brand deck, landscape or portrait, ready to save as PDF.
  • New
    A full site — Concept, Features, Product, Developers, Company — and a home at vaest.orions.agency.
  • Improved
    Monochrome geometric sigils and a unified night-prismatic identity across the app and the site.
  • Improved
    Resilience — Refine and Present fall back automatically if a model is briefly unavailable, so a pass never dead-ends.
  • Security
    Per-account row-level isolation — your workspace is yours alone. Usage and error logs are server-only.
  • Fixed
    The password-reset link now opens the set-password screen reliably, and branded auth emails replaced the plain defaults.
VÆST 1.1
June 2026

It becomes yours

  • New
    Taste memory — every approve and skip quietly biases the next suggestion, remembered per project.
  • New
    The client loop — share a read-only link, collect per-section comments, and turn a note into the revision with Apply with VÆST.
  • New
    Ø Think — a senior-creative provocation pass that pushes the work braver, each point anchored to the exact line.
  • New
    Settings hub — persona (Standard · Formal · Playful), reply-language lock, private-session mode, profile and avatar.
  • Improved
    Multi-canvas — distinct deliverables split into their own tabs, each editable on its own.
  • Improved
    A usage meter and fair-use quota so cost never surprises you.
VÆST 1.0
May 2026

The instrument

  • New
    The document canvas — brief and files become one sharp, sectioned, editable document. Not a chat — a deliverable.
  • New
    Refine — a holistic taste audit for inconsistency, redundancy and logic, approve one point at a time.
  • New
    Vision — moodboards reach the model as real images, so palette, type and composition actually inform the work.
  • New
    Versions, word-by-word diff, 30-step undo, and a 30-day trash — nothing good is ever lost.
  • New
    Export to Word, PDF and rich text.
