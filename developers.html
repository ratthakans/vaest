Developers · Guide

How to drive VÆST.

A working guide from a blank brief to a document you hand off — and the shortcuts that make it fast. No install, no config: it runs in the browser.

The workflow

Brief to hand-off, step by step

Start a session & write the brief

Hit New and describe the work in plain language — what you need, the context, who it's for. Attach files (docx, pdf, xlsx, csv, md, html, images) by clicking Attach or dropping them in. Images become real visual references. Long pastes turn into tiles automatically.

Spark in the Idea sandbox (optional)

Below the brief, use Idea to riff. Throw options, paste a ChatGPT/Midjourney fragment, argue a concept. Promote any good reply with → brief. Everything here feeds the next step as curated raw material.

Summing → the document

Press Summing (or ⌘↵). VÆST crystallizes the brief, files and ideas into one sectioned document, streaming live. If the work spans distinct deliverables it splits into canvas tabs. Toggle + Refined first to auto-run the taste check right after.

Edit on the canvas

Edit any section directly. Highlight a sentence for the toolbar — Shorten · Formal · Expand or an open Ask VÆST…. ⌘A selects just the section you're in. Add more files anytime with Add files; they merge into the document.

Ø Think — push it bolder

Hit Ø Think for a Senior Creative pass — ODIN proposes braver moves, each anchored to a highlighted quote. Approve the ones you want (VÆST learns your taste), Skip the rest.

Refined — the quality gate

Hit Refined for a holistic audit — inconsistency, redundancy, logic. Approve findings one at a time or Approve all. Use View changes to see what moved. Save a version anytime; compare with Diff.

Share & collect feedback

Export → Share link gives the client a read-only view (they can pick paper or night mode, no login). Their comments appear as a badge in your rail. Hit Apply with VÆST to turn a note into a revision, then Resolve. Revoke the link when done.

Hand off

Export as Word, a print-ready PDF, rich text for Docs/Slack, or build a Presentation deck (landscape or portrait). Save as PDF from the print dialog.

Persona & language

Open Settings (gear, bottom-left, or ⌘K → “Settings”). Choose a persona — Standard / Formal / Playful — that governs tone on every call, and lock the reply language to Auto, ไทย or English. Set a project's voice & guidelines from the project menu; it applies to every session inside it.
