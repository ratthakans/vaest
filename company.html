The story

VÆST began as an internal instrument at ORIONS.Agency — a creative studio that ships taste to clients every day. The problem wasn't a lack of ideas. It was the hours a senior creative lost at 10pm, staring at a pile that had to become a direction: four client emails contradicting each other, a 40-page audit no one finishes, a spreadsheet nobody opens, a moodboard that loves a vibe but can't say why.

That work — the crystallizing — is where taste actually lives, and it's exactly what generic AI does badly. So we built the instrument we wanted: not a chatbot, but a document canvas that takes the chaos in and produces one sharp, sectioned, editable document — with a creative director's judgment carried through every step.

“Computation is nothing without taste.”

The name is a mark, not a word. The slash — / — is the cut a creative director makes: this stays, this goes. Everything in VÆST is built around that single act of judgment, and around remembering how your studio makes it.

What we believe

Four principles

01

Taste is the product

Speed is a commodity — every AI tool will be fast within a year. The thing worth building is judgment: choosing what to cut, not just rephrasing what's there. VÆST is built to sharpen the decision, not replace the decider.

02

The right mind for each job

One generic brain for everything is a compromise. SKALD the poet sparks the idea, ODIN the mastermind shapes and provokes it, NORRSKEN the light perfects it. Each phase gets the mind built for it — named for the old north.

03

It should become yours

Generic intelligence is smart the same way for everyone. Every approve and skip teaches VÆST your studio's taste — so it stops living in one senior's head and becomes something the whole team runs on.

04

Refuse the banal

The enemy isn't the other tool — it's AI slop. VÆST exists to make work more considered, more tasteful, more human, not to flood the world with more of the forgettable.
